## ðŸŽ iOS Installation (iPhone/iPad)

1. Open https://whispermatch.vercel.app in **Safari**
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow pointing up)
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Name it "WhisperMatch" and tap **Add**
5. The app icon will appear on your home screen!

**Note:** Must use Safari browser on iOS (Chrome/Firefox won't work)

## ðŸ¤– Android Installation

### Method 1: Chrome Browser
1. Open https://whispermatch.vercel.app in **Chrome**
2. Tap the menu (three dots) in the top right
3. Tap **"Add to Home screen"** or **"Install app"**
4. Confirm by tapping **Add** or **Install**
5. Done!

### Method 2: Install Banner
1. Visit the site in Chrome
2. After 30 seconds, a banner will appear at the bottom
3. Tap **"Install"** on the banner
4. The app will be added to your home screen

## ðŸ”§ Troubleshooting

**"Add to Home Screen" option missing?**
- Make sure you're using a supported browser (Safari on iOS, Chrome on Android)
- Some browsers don't support PWA installation

**App not appearing after install?**
- Check all home screen pages
- Try searching for "WhisperMatch" in your app drawer

**Want to uninstall?**
- iOS: Long press the icon > Remove App
- Android: Long press > Uninstall or App Info > Uninstall
